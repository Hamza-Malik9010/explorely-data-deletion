Explorely Data Deletion Request

At Explorely, we value your privacy and want to ensure you have control over your personal information. If you wish to delete your data associated with your Explorely account, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Step 1: Log into your Explorely account.

Step 2: Visit your account settings page.

Step 3: Locate the "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion" option.

Step 4: Confirm your request. You may be asked to verify your identity or provide your account password to ensure the security of your request.

Once you have completed these steps, Explorelyâ€™s support team will process your data deletion request. Please note that the deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

What Happens When You Delete Your Data

When your data deletion request is processed, we will remove or anonymize your personal information so that it can no longer be used to identify you. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Your account details (username, email address, profile information)
  • Your travel histories, preferences, and reviews
  • Any other personally identifiable information associated with your Explorely account

Please be aware that certain non-personal information or aggregated data (which cannot identify you) may be retained for analytical, security, or legal compliance purposes.

Revoking Deletion Requests

If you change your mind before the deletion is finalized, you may contact us to cancel the request. After your data is deleted, the process cannot be reversed, and your information will be permanently removed.
